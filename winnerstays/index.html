Rise to The Top

Winner Stays provides an easy and interactive way of tracking pick up sports games in your local area.

Features

Real Time Updates

Don't worry, we will update the game page in real time, displaying which teams are currently on the court or field as well as which teams are waiting to play.

Join Local Teams

Create or Join local teams with your friends and try to climb the ranks in your area as we track your record.

Pick Your Sport

Currently we have 6 sports to choose from, Basketball, Soccer, Tennis, Baseball, Softball, and Volleyball.

How it works

1

Sign up

Create an account and username that will be used to help connect with friends and other players in your area.

2

Join Teams

You can either create a new team and/or join a team with people in your local area. Team records will be tracked to make things even more interesting!

3

Find Games

Find games or create new games. All games are held with a Winner Stays on the court or field format.

4

Watch Live Updates

Once you successfully have joined a live game, the game page will display live updates of which teams are playing as well as the order of the teams waiting!!
